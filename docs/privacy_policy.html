Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 13, 2026

Summary: Savage Timer does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All information stays on your device.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Savage Timer ("the App", "we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use our mobile application available on the Apple App Store and Google Play Store. Savage Timer is developed by Sol-cito ("the Developer").

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data the App accesses, how it is used, and your rights regarding that data.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. Savage Timer is a fully offline application that does not require an internet connection, user accounts, or registration of any kind.

The App does not collect, store, or transmit:

  • Personal identifiers (name, email address, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or telemetry data
  • Photos, contacts, or calendar data
  • Health or fitness data
  • Financial or payment information

3. Locally Stored Data

The App stores your timer configuration preferences locally on your device using the operating system's standard local storage mechanism (SharedPreferences on Android, UserDefaults on iOS). This data includes:

  • Round duration setting
  • Rest duration setting
  • Number of rounds
  • Last-seconds alert preference and threshold
  • Savage level selection (motivation intensity)
  • Volume level
  • Motivational sound on/off preference

This data is stored exclusively on your device, is never transmitted over the internet, and is never accessible to the Developer or any third party. You can delete this data at any time by clearing the App's data through your device settings or by uninstalling the App.

4. Device Permissions

The App requests the following device permissions solely for core functionality:

  • Vibration – To provide haptic feedback during timer events (round start, round end, rest transitions).
  • Wake Lock – To keep the device awake while the timer is actively running, preventing the screen from sleeping during your workout.
  • Background Audio (iOS) / Foreground Service (Android) – To continue running the timer, playing audio alerts, and providing vibration feedback when the App is in the background.
  • Notifications (Android) – To display the timer status in the notification bar when the App is running in the background.

The App does not request access to your camera, microphone, location, contacts, calendar, photos, or any other sensitive device features.

5. Third-Party Services

Savage Timer does not integrate any third-party services, including but not limited to:

  • Analytics or tracking services (e.g., Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics, Mixpanel)
  • Advertising networks (e.g., Google AdMob, Facebook Ads)
  • Crash reporting services (e.g., Crashlytics, Sentry)
  • Social media SDKs
  • Any cloud storage or backend services

6. Data Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties because we do not collect any data. There are no circumstances under which your information would be disclosed, sold, traded, or transferred to outside parties.

7. Children's Privacy

Savage Timer does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age in your jurisdiction). The App is safe for users of all ages as it does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The App complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar regulations worldwide.

8. Data Security

Since the App does not collect or transmit data over the internet, there is no risk of data breach through our services. Your timer settings are stored locally using your device's built-in secure storage mechanisms and are protected by your device's own security features (passcode, biometrics, encryption).

9. International Users

Savage Timer can be used anywhere in the world. Since no data is collected or transmitted, there are no cross-border data transfer concerns. The App complies with:

  • GDPR (European Union General Data Protection Regulation)
  • CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act)
  • LGPD (Brazil's General Data Protection Law)
  • PIPEDA (Canada's Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act)
  • PIPA (South Korea's Personal Information Protection Act)

10. Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data, traditional data subject rights (access, correction, deletion, portability) are not applicable. However, you always have the right to:

  • Delete all locally stored preferences by clearing the App's data or uninstalling the App.
  • Contact us with any privacy-related questions or concerns.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. If we make material changes to how we handle user data (for example, if we add data collection features in a future version), we will notify users through an in-app notice and update this policy accordingly before collecting any data.

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
